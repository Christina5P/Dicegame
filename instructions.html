How to play:

Can you guess the dice choice of number? Make a bet what you think the number would be after rolling the dice. You can decide the risk by choose a value.

  • 10 euro bet - you can pick 3 numbers to compare with the dice.
  • 50 euro bet - you pick 2 numbers.
  • 100 euro bet -you pick 1 number.

    • Click on a value and after that you can choose your numbers by clicking on them.
    When you have made your choices, its time to roll the dice by click on it
    If the dots on the dice is the same as one of the number you picked,
    you win the game and the value is yours!
    If its not, you lose and just try again.

    Good luck!
